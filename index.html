The Continuous Loop

Runs hundreds of times per second. Every robot follows this same cycle.

↻ Repeat — never stops while the robot runs
5-Step Process
01
Sense — Cameras, LiDAR, GPS, touch & temperature
02
Think — Obstacle? Path? Stop or go?
03
Plan — Path, speed & movement sequence
04
Act — Motors & servos execute precisely
05
Feedback — Constant self-correction in real time

Robotics = automation in the physical world.

Your trading tool analyzes data. A robot acts on it.
